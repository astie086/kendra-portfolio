My name is Kendra Herrera, growing up I have always pursued art as a passion and that hasn’t changed. I want to be your Artist for your next project! After working for 10 years in customer service in various market places I have always strived to make every customer happy in every kind of situation. Now I’m starting my own company with the same attitude and positive energy to my art design. I have created logo designs, graphic art, traditional art, story boards and customized clothing in many art styles such as; stipple, Van Gogh style, Anime/Cartoon, and my specialty realism. I live to create art in many shapes and forms.

I specialize in realistic color portraits because of the challenge. I enjoy the attention to detail that is required to make photo quality portraits come to life. This is where my passion for art started, by pushing myself to get that much detail with a pen/pencil/charcoal/pastel/paint and now graphic design. The smallest detail can make or ruin a portrait, that makes the finished piece all that much more rewarding. These small things that I think are often taken for granted or overlooked I find a humbling pleasure in bringing them to light. I believe it’s my love of challenges I was able to thrust myself into graphic design. Using my photoshop/adobe experience that I gained working for an e-commerce company, I have been able to create sleek and standout designs for company logos and eye-catching illustrations for company website pages. Graphic Design has given my art limitless capabilities and I’m excited to show it to the world.

I can charm any creative media and I want to help you make your ideas come to life.
